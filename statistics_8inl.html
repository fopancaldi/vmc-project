Detailed Description

Definition of the templated statistics algorithms.

Authors
Lorenzo Fabbri, Francesco Orso Pancaldi

Contains the definitions of the templated statistics algorithms declared in the header. Among the helper functions used in said definitions, the templated ones are also defined here, while the non-templated ones are in the .inl file.
