Statistical methods header.

Authors
Lorenzo Fabbri, Francesco Orso Pancaldi

Contains the declarations of statistical analysis algorithms that are meant to be called by the user. Does NOT contain the descritpions of said algorithms. To improve readability, the implementation of the templated functions is in the .inl file.
