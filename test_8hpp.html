Finds a point where the potential is large.

Parameters
wavefThe wavefunction
paramsThe variational parameters
potThe potential
boundsThe region in which the search for the peak will be done
numPointsHow many points will be sampled in the search
genThe random generator
Returns
The positions of the peak

Randomly chooses 'numPoints' points in the region and where the potential is largest, but the wavefunction is not too small. The latter is done to avoid having the wavefunction be 'nan', which breaks the VMC update algorithms.
